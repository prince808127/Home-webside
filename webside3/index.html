Why Choose Prefab

Features & Benefits

⏱️

Time-Effectiveness

80%–90% of the construction is completed inside a factory, which mitigates the risk of weather delays, allowing projects to be completed in half the time of traditional construction.

💰

Cost-Effectiveness

Because construction of modular buildings can occur simultaneously with the site and foundation work, projects are more cost-effective than traditional construction.

🔄

Flexibility

Flexibility and relocatability of modular components add to the cost-effectiveness over time. Their durable, secure and relocatable characteristics make them ideal for both temporary and permanent applications.

🏆

Quality

Loom Crafts utilizes state-of-the-art facilities that integrate best practices in design and workflow. Modular buildings are required to meet the same building codes as traditional construction.

🌿

Sustainability

The fully controlled fabrication process inside the factory generates less waste, creates fewer site disturbances and allows for tighter construction.

🛡️

Risk Reduction

The indoor construction environment reduces the risks of accidents and related liabilities for workers, ensuring a safer construction process.

Innovation

Technology & Design

01

Building Systems

LOOM CRAFTS uses the best dry technologies, already tested and available on the market, to design modular buildings with standard components. The structural components of the floor, wall and roof are totally prefabricated by Loom Crafts as we are experts in the processing, with numerically controlled machines, and assembly of the materials.

02

The Building's Configuration

LOOM CRAFTS promotes modularity in the construction sector: buildings will be no longer designed, but configured with standard components, that are produced in the factory. The shape, the function and every surface, internal and external, are customizable: there are no limits to the imagination!

03

Product Distribution

Any prefab component is delivered directly to the construction site and installed rapidly; thanks to an error-proof joint systems and mechanical fastenings. It is essential to involve any specialized actor, in order to guarantee environmentally friendly products, quick mounting, design and high quality buildings.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cost of prefab modular homes in India?+
The cost of a prefab modular home in India depends on the size, design, and finishes. At Loom Crafts, our single-room prefab cottages and glamping pods start from just ₹7.25 lakh, while 1 BHK modular cottages range from ₹11.8 lakh to ₹28 lakh, 2 BHK prefab homes start at ₹23 lakh, and 3 & 4 BHK villas go up to ₹98 lakh.
How long does it take to build a prefab modular house?+
Unlike traditional construction which can take years, our prefab modular cottages are factory-made and ready to install in just 45–90 days depending on size. Single-room pods and studios are ready in 45 days, 1 & 2 BHK homes in 60 days, and large 3 & 4 BHK villas in 75–90 days.
Are prefab homes suitable for all locations and terrains?+
Yes, our modular homes are designed for all terrains in India — whether it's flat farmland, hilly slopes, coastal regions, or forest retreats. We've delivered projects in Goa, Kerala, Coorg, Shimla, Manali, Rishikesh, Lonavala, Jaipur, and Dehradun, as well as urban locations like Delhi NCR, Pune, Bangalore, Hyderabad, and Chennai.
Can prefab modular homes be customized?+
Absolutely. Every Loom Crafts home is custom-built to suit your land, lifestyle, and budget. You can choose from A-Frame, BarnHouse, ModAlpine, Vista, PodVilla, Cabana, and Concept Home designs, along with roofing styles, cladding materials, interiors, and finishes.
Do prefab modular cottages meet Indian building codes?+
Yes, all our prefab modular homes are designed in compliance with Indian standards (IS codes) for structural strength, wind load, seismic safety, and durability. Our homes are engineered with STAAD-Pro structural analysis and built to withstand earthquakes, heavy rains, and winds up to 180 km/hr.
Are prefab homes eco-friendly and energy-efficient?+
Yes, prefab construction is sustainable and environmentally friendly. Our modular cottages use recyclable materials, energy-efficient insulation, and zero-wastage construction methods. This reduces your carbon footprint while ensuring year-round comfort in hot, cold, or humid climates.
Do you make wooden homes?+
We deliver the wooden cottage look using wood-look claddings over an LGSF structure (termite-proof, weather-ready). This gives you the beauty and warmth of a wooden home without the maintenance concerns.
What warranty and lifespan do your prefab homes offer?+
Our prefab homes come with a 20-year warranty covering structure and leakage, and they have a design life of over 50 years with regular maintenance.
